  <h3 id="About-Me">About Me</h3>

  <p> I am a Research Fellow at <a href="https://openphilanthropy.org/"target="_blank">Open Philanthropy</a>, where I help to find new cause areas with the Global Health & Wellbeing team. On the side, I also co-host the <a href="https://www.hearthisidea.com/"target="_blank">Hear This Idea</a> podcast, where <a href="https://www.finmoorhouse.com/"target="_blank">Fin Moorhouse</a> and I interview guests on "new thinking in philosophy, the social sciences, and effective altruism". </p>

    <p> Previously, I was a research scholar at Oxford's <a href="https://www.fhi.ox.ac.uk/"target="_blank">Future of Humanity Institute</a>, a research assistant at Oxford's <a href="https://www.smithschool.ox.ac.uk/"target="_blank">Smith School of Enterprise and the Environment</a>, and a policy advisor at the UK's <a href="https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/office-for-artificial-intelligence"target="_blank">Office for AI</a>. Before that, I did a bachelors in economics at the University of Cambridge. For more, check out my <a href="projects/Righetti_CV.pdf"target="_blank">CV</a> (and <a href="projects/Righetti_CV_Failures.pdf"target="_blank">CV of Failures</a>).</p> 

    <p> I consider myself part of the <a href="https://www.effectivealtruism.com/"target="_blank">effective altruism</a> and <a href="http://longtermism.com/"target="_blank">longtermism</a> communities.</p>
